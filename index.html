About Me

Yamkela Lusithi is an Digital Learning Developer, with a strong focus on designing and developing learning interfaces that improve and support digital literacy, learner engagement, and academic achievement. Educational Philosophy

I believe that technology should not replace teaching, but rather extend its impact. As a Digital Learning Developer, I focus on learner-centered, inclusive, and evidence-based practices that integrate digital tools seamlessly into teaching and learning.

My philosophy is that effective learning must be guided by pedagogy, not novelty. I aim to empower learners by using technology to cultivate digital literacy, critical thinking, and lifelong learning skills while ensuring accessibility for all students.

Research in Educational Technology

My research investigates how technology impacts literacy, digital competence, and learner engagement. Key areas include:

  • Blended learning in language education.
  • Gamification for literacy development.
  • EdTech tools for differentiated instruction.
